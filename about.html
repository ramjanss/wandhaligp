About Gram Panchayat Wandhali

Village Introduction

Wandhali is a rural village located in Maharashtra, under Warora Taluka of Chandrapur District. The village falls under the administrative jurisdiction of the local Gram Panchayat and is part of the Vidarbha region of eastern Maharashtra.

The village economy is primarily agriculture-based, with most residents engaged as cultivators and agricultural labourers. Major crops grown in the region typically include cotton, soybean, and other seasonal crops common to Chandrapur district.

Wandhali reflects the typical characteristics of rural Maharashtra — agricultural livelihood, close-knit community life, and governance through the Panchayati Raj system. Basic facilities such as primary education, electricity, drinking water supply, and road connectivity are available as per government rural development records.
